About us Hoop Tribe is a basketball training program that would give people that want to learn and be great at basketball opportunities. Hoop Tribe would be helpful for beginners to love the sport basketball, but not just love it but also be great at it. Hoop Tribe will teach them skills that can be apply in the game, Hoop Tribe will also enhance your stamina and agility. Hoop Tribe will not only be helpful for beginners but also to those players that have been playing for a long time, Hoop Tribe will help you enhance skills in game.
Hard Maple Court Our court is made from hard maple which reduce slips and accidents that may cause players injury, our court is well maintained and cleanse daily.
High-end Equipments Our facility consists of high-end equipments that will benefit players and help them enhance their skills.
Electrolyte Beverages Our facility has free bevarages that would help our players hydrate when they are tired from the training, they could choose from water, Gatorade or other drinks that are available.
